Kwai Group Chat

I worked as the UX Design Intern at Kwai in 2018, focusing on the core App, Kwai, a short video social networking app with a billion users and 160 million DAU (Daily Active Users). I went through the whole product process from the discussion of initial ideas with the product manager to the final shipment of the group chat feature and a series of features related.
